## The soft layers that do the most

The fastest, cheapest way to change a room's whole mood is the textiles.

**Taupe floral linen curtains.** Airy linen-blend panels printed with a delicate trailing floral. They filter daylight into a soft, diffused glow instead of darkening the room, and bring that quiet vintage-garden pattern that makes a space feel calm and collected — a whole new mood in an afternoon.

**A set of four corduroy pillow covers.** Chunky ribbed corduroy in warm layered neutrals — cream, camel and cocoa. Because it's just the covers, you can restyle an entire sofa for the price of one cushion. Hidden zippers, machine washable, endlessly mix-and-match.
