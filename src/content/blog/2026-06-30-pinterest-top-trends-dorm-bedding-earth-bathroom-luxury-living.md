## 2. Earth Tone Bathroom Designs — up 300%

Warm, earthy, spa-at-home — the bathroom trend that just keeps growing. The **sandstone ribbed ceramic set**
(soap dispenser, holder, cup and dish) instantly warms up a vanity with that stone-look, earth-tone finish.
Want the whole room to match in one order? The **coordinated beige set** brings the shower curtain, soft

## 3. Minimalist Luxury Living Spaces — up 200%

Quiet luxury, minus the price tag. A matched **pair of gold lamps** under black grid shades gives a sofa or a
set of nightstands that high-end symmetry and a soft, warm glow. And nothing says *quiet luxury* like
**moss green velvet curtains** — rich, room-darkening, with a sheen that drapes into heavy, expensive-looking
folds and plays beautifully with cream, wood and brass.

## 4. Cozy Reading Chairs — up 200%

The corner everyone wants: a deep, soft chair you can disappear into with a book. The **cozy lazy chair +
ottoman** has a little side pocket for your book and phone and turns an empty corner into a reading nook. The
**plush reading chair + ottoman** adds a throw pillow and a tuck-away footstool, compact enough for a small
apartment or bedroom — pull it up to a window with a blanket and it's the comfiest seat in the house.

## 5. Cozy Work Office — up 70%

Working from home doesn't have to mean a sterile desk. The **vintage floral desk mat** covers the whole
desktop in soft cottagecore color (and looks even better with a cat napping on it) — the cheapest way to make
a workspace feel cozy and styled. Finish the look with a **stack of decorative books**: four faux designer
volumes with clean neutral spines that add height and that collected, intentional feel to any shelf, console
or desk.
